Act 2

Citadel of Song

The Citadel

As the Grand Gate before the Last Judge finally opened, this is the largest area in the game. The Cogwork Dancers can be fought here, right inside the first Cogwork Core room.

Underworks

Home to Pharloom's very own mimimum wage workers (literally, as the enemies here drop little to no rosaries), and also home to the Clawline.

Cogwork Core

A sub-area of the Citadel, and one of the few ways to access Whispering Vaults and to get up to The Cradle.

Whispering Vaults

A sub-area of the Citadel and home to flamboyant drama king, Trobbio.

Bilewater

An extension of the Sinner's Road's muckmaggot water, Groal the Great can be fought here. This is also an alternative route to Phantom's Exhaust Organ.

The Slab

If you got kidnapped in Bilewater or in the previously mentioned areas Greymoor and Deep Docks, you get sent here. Escape by gathering the three keys scattered around The Slab.

Mount Fay

After escaping The Slab, you gain access to Mount Fay. Traverse it and you will find the Faydown Cloak which grants Hornet the ability to double jump.

Putrified Ducts

An area right next to the Memorium, the Putrified Ducts can be turned into the final home of the Flea Caravan.

The Cradle

The last ascension and the last two major fights in Act 2 occur here, with Lace once again and her mother, Grand Mother Silk.
