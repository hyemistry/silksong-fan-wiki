Characters and NPCs

Hornet

Battle Cry (Hollow Knight)

Speaking

The princess-protector of Hallownest, daughter of the Pale King, raised by the three queens: birthed by Herrah the Beast, raised by the White Lady, and trained by Hive Queen Vespa.

She was abducted by the pilgrims of Pharloom on the orders of Grand Mother Silk in order to rehome her power of silk regeneration through soul.

Lace

Laughing

Speaking

A being made out of Grand Mother Silk’s threads who yearned for her attention. However, as she thought Grand Mother Silk preferred Hornet, she fights her twice in order to prevent her from ascending to The Cradle.

Shakra

Singing

Speaking

A wandering cartographer mapping out areas around Pharloom in pursuit to her long lost master. Whenever found or summoned in Bone Bottom/Bellhart, a map of the latest area being explored can be bought from her.

She is also a boss fight.

Sherma

Singing

Speaking

A pure-hearted pilgrim on a journey to the Citadel.

Bell Beast

Purring

Hornet's Call

A beast first found stuck in the silk threads of The Marrow. At first, she is hostile—once Hornet knocks some sense into her, she becomes both a companion and the counterpart of Hollow Knight’s transportation Stags.

Pebb

Singing

A pilgrim who temporarily resides at Bone Bottom. He is selling goods that can be useful in the long run.

After buying all of his items, he eventually leaves Bone Bottom to go on a trek to the Citadel. However, later on in The Marrow, he can be seen possessed by the Haunting. Killing him drops 50 rosaries.

Pilby

Singing

A pilgrim who temporarily resides at Bone Bottom. His name can be known if Hornet talks enough with him.

Resting at a bench after defeating the first Skull Tyrant fight in Act 1 will cause him to get run over by it on the second fight, killing him.

Ballow

Singing

A miner in Deep Docks and the husband of Forge Daughter. He is also in charge of the Diving Bell which can be used to proceed to The Abyss.

Seamstress

Singing

One of the last members of The Order of the Pinstress that is still alive today. She gives Hornet a quest once she reaches her hut in the Far Fields, which in return rewards her the Drifter’s Cloak.
