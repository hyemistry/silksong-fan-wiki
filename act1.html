Act 1

Pharloom

Moss Grotto

After Hornet fell from the broken bridge of Blasted Steps, she first ends up here—this is the starting area of Silksong. Traverse this area further to fight Moss Mother, the tutorial boss (who does 2 mask damage per hit). Afterwards, Hornet can ascend to Bone Bottom and its succeeding area, The Marrow.

The Marrow

After fighting Moss Mother, Hornet gets access to Bone Bottom and The Marrow, all situated at the bottom of the Blasted Steps' fall. Bell Beast can be fought here to gain access to the Bellways, a fast travel system.

Hunter's March

Home of the hunter ants, the Beast Crest, and Savage Beastfly, the most annoying boss in the game according to me, the one and only Editor.

Deep Docks

A place full of heat and lava, Lace can be first fought here.

Far Fields

Fields full of greenery similar to Greenpath, the Fourth Chorus haunts you after obtaining the Drifter's Cloak.

Greymoor

The greyest of all areas, Moorwing can be fought right next to the entrance to Bellhart.

Bellhart

A temporary resting area for pilgrims hailing from the bottom of Pharloom, and the halfway point of Act 1. Bellhart is to be saved from first defeating Sister Splinter to gain access to Widow's fight, which grants Hornet the Needolin.

Shellwood

Home to the vegetation granted by their heart, Shellwood is also home to Sister Splinter who took over the area's rule.

Blasted Steps

This area is one of the few paths to leave Pharloom and return to Hallownest. The Last Judge awaits at the end, who will fight you if you are not of pure heart. Defeating the Last Judge grants you access to the Citadel.

Also, according to me, the Editor who had a first-hand experience, the run back to the Last Judge fight was one of the worst.

Sinner's Road

An alternative route to the Citadel, this area is full of maggots and flies. Phantom's mist can be found here, at the northwestern-most room. Traversing it grants you access to the Exhaust Organ, which also grants you access to her fight. Defeating her allows you to enter the Citadel without fighting the Last Judge.
