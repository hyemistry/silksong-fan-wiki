Act 3

Abyss

Deep Docks

Descend to the Deep Docks once more to gain access to the Diving Bell. This requires the player to have been defeated Forebrothers Signis & Gron.

The Abyss

Similar to the Abyss in the original Hollow Knight, this area is full of voidness. Grand Mother Silk and Lace are situated underneath the vast void ocean. Later on, Hornet must come back down here, however, she must obtain Silk Soar first, which is an ability to super jump, then escape the Abyss.

Nyleth's Shrine

The Pollen Heart must be obtained in Nyleth's shrine by defeating Shrine Guardian Seth and Nyleth herself.

Coral Tower

Located in the northwesternmost room of Sands of Karak, the Encrusted Heart must be obtained here by defeating Crust King Khann.

Far Fields

Located in the northeasternmost room of Far Fields by accessing it through Hunter's March, the Hunter's Heart must be obtained here by defeating Skarrsinger Karmelita.

Lost Verdania

Located in the east of the Craw Arena, Lost Verdania can only be accessed by playing the Elegy of the Deep to the Green Prince. A requirement to this is that Green Prince must be freed from his Sinner's Road cell. By defeating the Clover Dancers, the player will receive the Conjoined Heart.

Red Memory

Find the Everbloom in Hornet's past in Hallownest.

The Abyss

Return to the Abyss after obtaining the Everbloom and snap Lace out of her void possession under the void sea.
